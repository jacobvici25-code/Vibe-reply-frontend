Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Welcome to Vibe Reply AI ("we", "us", or "our"), built by Demx. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service at vibe-reply-frontend.vercel.app.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Vibe Reply AI is a demo application. We want to be fully transparent:

  • We do NOT store any messages you paste into the app
  • We do NOT save your replies or conversation history
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or personal information
  • We do NOT require you to create an account
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties

2. How the App Works

When you paste a message and click "Generate Reply", your message is sent to our backend server to generate an AI reply. This message is processed in real time and is NOT stored anywhere after the reply is generated.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track general usage data such as the number of visitors, country of origin, and device type. This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

4. Advertising

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising policies at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Third Party Services

Our app uses the following third party services to function:

  • Groq API — for AI reply generation
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous usage tracking
  • Vercel — for website hosting
  • Render — for backend server hosting

6. Children's Privacy

Vibe Reply AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our app.
